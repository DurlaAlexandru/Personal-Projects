FAQ - Frequently asked questions:

Below are some answers to the questions you might have.

This might help you solve the issue before you reach the Support Departament.

Try a reboot of the system! It's important to try a reboot before. After the reboot you can try running a windows update scan => Type "Windows Update settings" and press "Check for updates" - maybe it's an update problem.

Checking the connection is key! First - look if you are connected or not. If you are not connected, try to manually reconect. If you are connected but you don't have access to the internet, try to do a basic troubleshoot via RightClick on the internet icon and then press the "Troubleshoot problems" and follow any instructions there.

Equipment is delivered within a day or even less - depending on the equipment. You need to write us a ticket and specify what equipment the new joiner needs and also the first day at work of the new joiner.
